Mobile app concept: tracking deliveries across marketplaces

Contribution

A conceptual UX/UI project designed to simplify shopping across multiple delivery services.

(1)

Have to switch

Users have to switch between five different delivery apps, searching for the same product in each one. There is no single place to compare prices or track all their orders in one view.

(2)

Solution

One app that brings together all stores and delivery services. Search for products, compare prices, and track all your orders in one place — without switching between apps.

(3)

Design principles

A card-based structure where each product and order is a separate card, letting users scan information instantly without unnecessary text.

A minimal interface that shows only what is relevant in the current context, removing visual noise.

A unified navigation system that brings all services into one place.

(4)

Position

A space where users control their shopping experience — not the other way around.

(5)

Tracking

A single dashboard that aggregates all deliveries from different marketplaces in one place, letting users track every order in real time without switching between apps — with clear status updates and a unified view of the entire delivery process.
