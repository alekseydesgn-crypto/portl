Nocturne is a four-person practice based in Paris and Lisbon. We partner with founders, design leaders, and cultural institutions on digital products and the brands that hold them up.

Every engagement begins the same way: a long conversation, a sharp brief, and the patience to do fewer things, better. We believe restraint is a feature.

03 ⏤ Capabilities

Five practices, one studio. We rarely do just one.

Most engagements braid two or three of these together — an identity that ships as a product, a product that ships as an editorial. We staff for the whole arc.

⏤ 01

Product & UX design

Interfaces with intent. From the first wireframe to the last micro-interaction — we design products that feel inevitable in the hand.

ResearchIAInteractionDesign systemsPrototyping
⏤ 02

Web development

Editorial sites, configurators, and storefronts engineered to be fast, accessible, and quietly impressive.

⏤ 03

Brand identity

Marks, type systems, and editorial vocabularies for houses that prefer not to shout.

⏤ 04

Motion design
